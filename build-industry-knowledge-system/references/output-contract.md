## Full-System Acceptance Criteria

- All five modules have usable artifacts.
- The database dimensions are connected, not duplicated silos.
- Major claims and current metrics have source links and dates.
- Deep dives distinguish fact and inference.
- Content analysis defines sample and metric limitations.
- Knowledge map includes relationships and opportunity prioritization.
- Intelligence workflows can be repeated by another person.
