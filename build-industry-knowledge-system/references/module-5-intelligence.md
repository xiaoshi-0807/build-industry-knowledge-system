# Module 5: Continuous Intelligence

## Operating Model

Build four connected outputs:

| Output | Cadence | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| `sources.md` | Quarterly review | Curated signal sources |
| `weekly-competitor-update.md` | Weekly | Detect company and offer changes |
| `trending-log.md` | Daily | Detect fast-growing content and narratives |
| `weekly-report.md` | Weekly | Convert signals into decisions |

## Source Portfolio

Include:

- YouTube or long-form expert channels.
- X/LinkedIn or fast expert commentary.
- Newsletters/trade publications.
- Reddit/forums/communities.
- Regulators, standards, datasets, journals, or filings.

Rank sources by decision value, not only audience size. Record audience size, content direction, update frequency, reason to follow, and observation date where applicable.

## Competitor Monitoring

Weekly checks:

- New products/services.
- New collections/categories/pages.
- New blog/resources.
- Pricing, bundles, subscriptions, promotions.
- Keyword and claim changes.
- Navigation, channel, partnership, and trust changes.

Maintain snapshots and compare against the previous successful run. Never treat a failed crawl as “no change.”

## Content Trend Tracking

Daily tracking:

- Fastest like/reaction growth.
- Fastest share/repost growth.
- Fastest view/impression growth.
- Emerging terms, claims, formats, and audience identities.

Archive by date and category. Compare the same content across at least two time points.

## Weekly Report

Answer:

1. What changed in the industry?
2. Which products and content broke out?
3. Which companies made meaningful moves?
4. What is the most actionable opportunity?
5. What should be done next week?

Each item should include source, importance, implication, confidence, and next action.
